About Jealouss

Jealouss brings an innovative Scratch Card Game to the Chhath Puja Mela in Bihar, one of the largest and most vibrant festivals in the region. Our interactive game attracts thousands of participants who enjoy the thrill of scratching to reveal exciting prizes.

For businesses, this presents an unparalleled advertising opportunity. We display your brand on our large screens throughout the event, reaching a massive audience in a festive and engaging environment.

Our platform combines traditional festival celebrations with modern marketing techniques, creating a memorable experience for both participants and advertisers.

Located at Dhumnagar Chowk, Narkatiaganj, we're at the heart of the Chhath Puja celebrations, ensuring maximum visibility for your brand.
